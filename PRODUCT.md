# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Teaching staff, management, and high-level administrators at a university. They are university employees seeking to apply for, track, and approve leave requests efficiently.

## Product Purpose
An automated, hierarchical Employee Leave Management System (ELMS) that tracks leave quotas and dynamically routes requests up the chain to final approval. Success means a fast, transparent, and frictionless process for both applicants and high-level approvers.

## Positioning
A dynamic, automated approval hierarchy that maps exact reporting chains from teachers to the Vice Chancellor.

## Brand Personality
Professional, authoritative, and frictionless (like a high-end enterprise tool).

## Anti-references
Not like a generic, clunky, 90s-era university or government portal.

## Design Principles
- **Clear Hierarchy:** Visual layout must mirror the organizational structure and dynamic approval chains.
- **Frictionless Approvals:** Reduce cognitive load for high-level approvers by surfacing only the most critical information.
- **Institutional Authority:** The interface must feel secure, official, and trustworthy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard enterprise accessibility (high contrast, keyboard navigable, clear typography).
